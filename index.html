FEATURED PEST ID:

Lady Bug Larvae

Before you mistake this little critter for a pest to be exterminated with extreme prejudice, hold up, wait a minute, this is an ally! This albino Godzilla-esque bug is the spawn of the ladybug and one of it's favorite foods are aphids so roll out the red carpet for these little warriors which can help you fight the war against the true pests!
