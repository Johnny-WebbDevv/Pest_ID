Who am I and why did I make this website?

I'm John Williams and during my agricultural journey, predominantly in my own backyard, I've come across so many things on plants that were just peculiar and sometimes concerning so this is my way to assist anyone who might want to identify weird stuff on their plants.

I've been exposed to gardening from the time I was a small child when my father had some seasonings and short crops planted in cut blue barrels in the front of the yard as we've always had something planted ever since.

As a probably great scientific mind once said:

"Let's get schwifty!"
